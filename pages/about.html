Our Story

Gold Bottom Ent. LLC was founded with a simple belief: talent deserves a champion. Whether you're a soul singer captivating audiences with your voice, a designer bringing brands to life, or a developer building the next great application — you deserve professional management, strategic direction, and a team that believes in your vision.

Born from over 20 years of experience in the entertainment industry and a passion for technology, Gold Bottom Ent bridges the gap between Entertainment and Enterprise. We don't just manage talent — we build businesses, protect intellectual property, and create sustainable careers.

Based in the Washington, DC metropolitan area, we serve artists, creatives, and businesses nationwide. Our dual-brand approach means we understand both the creative soul and the business brain — and we bring both to every project.

Mission

To elevate talent, protect creative work, and build sustainable businesses through professional management and innovative technology.

Vision

To be the most trusted name in entertainment management and enterprise solutions — where every artist and every client feels like the priority.

Values

Excellence in everything. Integrity in every deal. Transparency with every client. Creativity without limits. Results that speak for themselves.

Why "Ent"?

Our name is intentional. "Ent" stands for two things:

Entertainment

The heart of our business — managing artists, booking shows, producing music, and building creative careers. If you perform, create, or inspire — we're your team.

Enterprise

The brain of our business — developing websites, building apps, consulting on technology, and solving digital problems. If you need tech solutions — we build them.

Both verticals share the same DNA: excellence, integrity, and results. Whether we're booking a sold-out show or deploying a web application, the standard is the same.

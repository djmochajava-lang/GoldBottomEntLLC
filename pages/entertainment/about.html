Our Story

Gold Bottom Entertainment is the creative arm of Gold Bottom Ent LLC — built from over two decades of experience in the music and entertainment industry. We don't just manage artists; we build careers, create moments, and leave lasting impressions.

Founded by Jeffery Ponder, CISSP, a lifelong musician, performer, and entrepreneur, Gold Bottom Entertainment was born from a simple belief: every artist deserves a team that works as hard as they do. From booking local venues to landing international radio play, we've been in the trenches and know what it takes to succeed.

Based in the Washington, DC metropolitan area, we serve artists, musicians, and creative professionals who are serious about their craft and ready to take it to the next level.

Mission

To provide artists with world-class management, booking, and creative services that turn raw talent into sustainable careers.

Vision

To be the entertainment management company that independent artists trust to amplify their art and protect their legacy.

Values

Authenticity. Hustle. Loyalty. Creative freedom. Results that speak louder than promises.

About Gold Bottom Entertainment

The story behind the music, the artists, and the mission.

Our Story

Gold Bottom Entertainment was born from a passion for music and a commitment to the artists who create it. With over 20 years of experience in the entertainment industry, we understand what it takes to build a sustainable career in music — and we're here to make it happen.

From artist management and live performance booking to music production and creative direction, we provide the full spectrum of services that artists need to thrive. Our approach is hands-on, relationship-driven, and results-oriented.

Based in the Washington, DC metropolitan area, we serve artists and performers nationwide. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or an emerging talent, Gold Bottom Entertainment is your partner in building something that lasts.

Mission

To elevate talent, protect creative work, and build sustainable careers through professional management and genuine partnership.

Vision

To be the most trusted name in entertainment management — where every artist feels like the priority, not a number.

Values

Excellence in craft. Integrity in every deal. Transparency with every artist. Creativity without limits. Results that speak.

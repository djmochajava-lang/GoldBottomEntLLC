Our Story

Gold Bottom Enterprise is the technology arm of Gold Bottom Ent LLC, delivering web development, application design, and technology consulting services to businesses and organizations. We combine deep technical expertise with a genuine understanding of business needs.

What started as building tools for our entertainment management operations grew into a full-service enterprise division. We realized that the same principles that make great entertainment management — attention to detail, creative problem-solving, and relentless execution — make for exceptional technology solutions.

Based in the Washington, DC metropolitan area, we serve clients ranging from startups and small businesses to nonprofits and established companies. Every project gets the same level of dedication, whether it's a landing page or a full-stack application.

USPTO Trademark Filing

Register trademarks with the USPTO to protect brand names, slogans, and logos.

  • TEAS Plus: $250/class (strict requirements)
  • TEAS Standard: $350/class (flexible)
  • Search existing marks before filing
  • Must demonstrate use in commerce
